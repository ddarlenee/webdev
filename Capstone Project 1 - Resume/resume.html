Summary

I am a dedicated learner with aspirations to contribute to innovation and technology in Singapore. Ever since I started studying O Level Computing, I have found it relatively challenging yet intriguing because I am drawn to problem-solving. As I delve deeper, I realised there are many areas where computing can be applied. Applying it to support national security is a very meaningful cause to me as Singapore is my home where I grew up.

During the pandemic, I started to further my interest in technology by attending an Introduction to Programming Languages course by BuildingBloCS and expanded my knowledge of Python through my research project on synthetic aperture sonar. By processing data with Python, I have gained a deeper understanding of Python and hope to put my knowledge to use in software development projects. Having actively participated in competitions and projects such as Bebras, Olympiad, CRADLΣ, YSC, Elementz, and Kyoto ExP, I have also found joy in teamwork and leading projects.

In my free time, I study other programming languages such as CSS and HTML and have attended BuildingBloCS workshops on them to improve my understanding of the aesthetic aspects of web app development. I wish to learn more about the various programming languages and deepen my knowledge as well as hone my skills in software engineering and artificial intelligence.

Currently, on top of A level Computing, I am taking some courses on Datacamp and AI Singapore. In addition, I facilitated an introduction to python workshop during the June holidays this year to help those in the community who are interested in picking up coding.

I am open to feedback and willing to follow instructions while offering respectful suggestions.
